Featured Event

The 43rd Annual Prince Lot Hula Festival is the largest non-competitive hula celebration in Hawaii. It honors Prince Lot Kapuaiwa, who helped reprise hula in the 1850s and reigned as Kamehameha V from 1863 to 1872. The festival will also feature a Hawaiian craft fair, and cultural demonstrations.

Where? 364 S King St, Honolulu, HI 96813

When? July 18 9 am to 4 pm and July 19 9 am to 3 pm

Featured Community Service

Ka Papa Lo'i o Kanewai - Get dirty in the lo‘i every first Saturday of the month. Refreshments provided. Or you can organize a private work day with the lo‘i staff.

Contact Info: (808) 956-0640

Visit their website: manoa.hawaii.edu/hshk/ka-papa-loi-o-kanewai/
