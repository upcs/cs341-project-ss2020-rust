About Makaleha

Makaleha is an online tool to help locals and visitors embrace the beauty of Hawai'i by learning about the free opportunities and events in the islands. We are a government sponsored tool designed to allow an unbiased display of all the wonderful events and attractions that our island has to offer.

Help

Navigation

Account info: click the hamburger icon in the top right corner to log in, log out, sign up for an account (register), or write a review (instructions below). Homepage: click the picture above the description of a page and it will take you there. Also, use the navagation bar to go to a desired page. Other pages: Click the Makaleha header in top left corner to return to homepage. Use navagation bar to go to other pages.

Write a Review

Here are the steps to follow to write a review an entry:

  1. Press the hamburger icon in the top right corner of the page

  2. Click the 'Write a Review' button

  3. Fill in the empty boxes and select a star rating

  4. Press the 'Submit' button

Find Catelog of Favorited Attractions

To favorite an attraction to save to your account you must be logged in. Then you will browse one of the pages and click on a location icon on the map or a table entry. This will prompt a pop up on the map which has a star icon on it. Click the star icon in the top right corner of the map to add this attraction to your favorites.

To find your favorites you must be logged on. Then you click the hamburger icon in the top right corner of the screen. Click the dropdown option labeled 'account'. Once on the account page, select favorites from the options on the left and the website will display the attractions you have favorited.
